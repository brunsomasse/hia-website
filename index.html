Our Mission

Loss should never be
a life sentence.

When a husband or father is lost, a family's entire foundation can collapse overnight — income, housing, education, and security all at risk at once. Hope International Association exists to close that gap.

We are not a relief organization that disappears once the crisis fades from the headlines. We are a long-term partner in rebuilding — through scholarships, microfinance, healthcare access, and vocational training that help widows regain independence and give orphaned children a real path forward.

Every franc raised goes toward a person, a family, a future — never toward abstraction.

What We Do

Support that follows
real need.

Our programs are structured around the real, sequential needs of the families we serve — from emergency stabilization to lasting independence.

Emergency Relief

Housing support, food security, and urgent medical care when a family needs it most.

Education & Scholarships

School fees, supplies, and tuition for orphaned children — and continuing education for widows.

Microfinance & Livelihood

Small loans and business training that build lasting financial independence for widows.

Health Services

Access to medical care, screenings, and ongoing wellness support for families in need.

Vocational Training

Practical, locally relevant skills — tailoring, agriculture, trades — built around real opportunity.

Community & Mentorship

Support networks connecting widows and giving orphaned children mentors as they grow.
